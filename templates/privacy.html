Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account on Spendly, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and password. When you use the service, we store the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — so that we can display it back to you.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expenses, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorised personnel only. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or other operational needs. These services may have access to your data only to the extent necessary to perform their functions and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes. We do not integrate with any third-party advertising networks.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to request deletion of your data, or have concerns about how your information is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
